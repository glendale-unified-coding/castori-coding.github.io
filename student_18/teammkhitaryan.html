Henrikh Mkhitaryan

"Mkhitaryan is a blessing to Manchester United"

Henrikh Mkhitaryan is a proffesional footballer that used to play for Borussia Dortmund, but now plays for proffesional team Manchester United. He also plays for the Armenian National Team. He is an attacking Midfielder, who is capable of playing his two main positions as a Right Winger and a Right Midfielder. He is 27 years old, born on 21 January, 1989 in Yerevan, Armenia.
