What is SqueerkatHub?

SqueerkatHub is essentially a place for squids and meerkats, wherever they are, to have the oppurtunity to watch videos and look at pictures of themselves. If humans can do that all the time, why should two randomly picked species of animal be denied that right? (Although, they probably don't have computers, so...)
