Here are some facts you didn't know about dogs.......

  1. Dogs are left and right handed just like us
  2. Dogs' personalities often reflect upon their owners
  3. Scientists have discovered that dogs can dream just like humans
  4. Humans that spend time with dogs are more likely to have a reduced risk of depression than others

The pinpoints below will help you figure out if you are ready to get a dog or not.

  • Do you have the right amount of space for the type of dog you want to get, such as a fenced yard?
  • Do you have any time to spend with your companion? This will prevent conditions such as depression.
  • You have to walk the dog at least 2-3 times a day, do you have time for that?
  • And most importantly.........DO YOU WANT TO PICK UP THEIR POOP?

If you answered YES to 2 or more of the questions above....then you are ready to get a dog!!!!
